5 in 5

idea 1

Sensors detect when a dog is drinking water and send a message to the owner's phone.
The audience is the people who love the dog,
and they want to have some interaction with their puppy even if they leave home.
the inspiration from the monitor camera.

idea 2

It is a wearable mask that can be integrated into the mask to measure respiration.
We will demonstrate its functionality and accuracy with a p5
sketch that allows users to interact and visualize the body that keeps them alive while simultaneously understanding that they are in full control by interacting
with a game interface. The inspiration from the PS4 game controller.

idea 3

I wanted to construct an infinitely large "room" by
taking advantage of recursive virtual images. Inside the room would exist a world of light:
thousands of points of light would be orchestrated to
permeate and color the vast dull emptiness of the infinite space.
The inspiration from the japanese artist Yayoi Kusama.

idea 4

This project is about human relationships,
and I chose to express these emotions by using a water-lily flower to show interaction.
I want to give this artwork a sense of awareness like
human beings such as jealousy, angry, sad, pleasure, and lonely.
When I approach one of them, it will open the leaves beautifully, but at the same time,
other flowers will making the noise. The inspiration from the flower birthday candle.

idea 5

This is a game in which you control the balance of the board
through a controller to get the ball through the maze.
The audience is the children who love to play the game and the inspiration from the maze.
